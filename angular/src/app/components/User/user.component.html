To create an account in Graphs4Social is necessary to agree with our Privacy Policies.

When you create an account, we process your personal data, taking into account the following points:

- We store the data you provide us, such as, name, email, phone number and birth date to service control.

- We will keep your name and email to be able to identify you in our database.

- We use your email and phone number to be able to communicate with the client. This communication aims to the treatment of personal data and further changes in our Privacy Policies.

- In case of inativity by the client, the data will remain in the system for a period of one year. At the end of this period the data will be removed from the system.

At any time, it is possible for the customer to access their data (and may even change them), the way in which they are handled and their purpose.

The customer can also request the removal of certain data:

- If these are no longer needed for its purpose.

- When consent is withdrawn and there is no other legal basis for it.

- When you oppose the treatment and there are no legitimate interests prevailing.

- When personal data have been unlawfully processed.

- When personal data have to be erased in order to fulfill a legal obligation.

- When personal data have been collected in the context of providing information society services.
